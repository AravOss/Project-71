school library

School auditorium

school auditorium

One of the oldest places in the school, the auditorium is where school plays and rehersals are held.The St.xavier's school auditorium can hold up to 500 students at one time. This round amphitheater is one of the funnest places in the campus. this building is located in the northwest wing of the school. the auditorium overlooks the pool and the library.It was built in the year 1982
